Methodology

Six signals. One hundred points. No black box.

Every point in every score traces to a public filing at Companies House. The breakdown is the product — you can check our working, and you should.

Owner age · 35 pts

The calendar is still the strongest signal, but it is read properly now: the controlling owner from the PSC register where one is named, the oldest active director where not, ramping smoothly from 55 to 75 and beyond. Month-and-year of birth as the register provides it, never a full date. Age makes a company eligible; it proves nothing about intent.

Control tenure · 10 pts

The longest-serving active director's years at the table, from ten to twenty-five. Decades of unbroken control usually mean the founder is still holding the pen.

Control concentration · 10 pts

Who actually owns it, from the PSC register — reported in bands, with trusts, corporate holders and protected records, so it is scored as a confidence, never a yes/no. A 40% founder with passive family around the table is still a mandate.

No visible successor · 10 pts

If no director under 45 has been appointed in five years, nobody visible is being groomed for the chair. A successor may exist off the register — so this signal is deliberately modest.

Urgency events · 35 pts

Behaviour, not demography: directors leaving without replacement, a fresh auditor or registered-office change, a shortened accounting period, security satisfied with none created. Age creates the population; events pick this month's calls.

Transaction simplicity · 0–100

Starts at a hundred and subtracts for ownership layers, crowded cap tables, charge churn and status markers. Carrying ordinary bank security costs nothing. Complexity dampens a target's priority — it never deletes it, because a complicated sale is precisely when an adviser earns the fee.

Data confidence · 0–100

How much the register actually told us — owner age, ownership, filing history, charges. Accounts are not yet ingested, so confidence tops out at 85 by construction: what we don't know lowers the number, it is never guessed. Every company here has also passed a qualification gate: no strike-off markers, no corporate-group ownership, a live board.
